Welcome To St. John's

St. John's National Academy, founded in 2012, is a premier educational institution committed to fostering holistic development and nurturing global citizens. Nestled in the verdant landscapes of Salem, Tamil Nadu, our institution spans 15 acres and offers an environment that caters to the diverse needs of our students. Run by the Salem Diocese Society, we aim to imbue our students with natural, social, and gospel values, shaping them into responsible and value-driven individuals who can be the light and salt of the world. Our dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and innovative teaching methodologies create an ecosystem where students can explore, learn, and grow to their fullest potential.

Fr. S. Stanly
